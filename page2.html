Cats teach a kind of quiet attention. Not big attention—small attention. A cat sitting near the window. A cat sleeping in impossible positions. A cat staring at something you cannot see. A cat suddenly running like the floor is lava. Living with a cat means accepting its rules: it chooses distance, timing, and mood. Sometimes it follows me from room to room. Sometimes it ignores me completely. Sometimes it sits in the doorway like a soft checkpoint. Cats are everywhere online, but a personal cat page feels different: it’s not “cat content,” it’s one relationship repeated over time. (Click the text. Click the images.)
Cats are small, agile carnivorous mammals known for their sharp senses and independent yet affectionate nature, having lived as both skilled hunters and beloved companions alongside humans for thousands of years.
