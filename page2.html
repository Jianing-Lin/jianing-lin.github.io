page two / cat notes
Cats are small, agile carnivorous mammals known for their sharp senses and independent yet affectionate nature, having lived as both skilled hunters and beloved companions alongside humans for thousands of years. I wanted the second page to feel closer and more personal than the first one. The first page is more like an introduction. This page is more like staying in the room longer and noticing details.
Living with a cat means paying attention to very small actions. A cat sitting near the window. A cat sleeping in impossible positions. A cat staring at something that you cannot see. A cat jumping onto the bed exactly when you are trying to work. These moments are ordinary, but they build a kind of visual diary. Cats are everywhere online, but a personal cat website is different from just reposting cat content. This page is about one specific relationship between one human and one animal, built through repetition, observation, and daily life. That is why I wanted to use both images and text.
