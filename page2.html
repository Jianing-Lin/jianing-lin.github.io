Living with a cat means sharing your space with a small, independent personality. Cats spend a large part of their day sleeping—often between twelve and sixteen hours. But when they are awake, they become curious explorers. A moving shadow, a dangling string, or even a small sound can suddenly become the most important thing in the world. Although cats have a reputation for being aloof, many of them form deep bonds with their owners. They learn routines, recognize voices, and sometimes follow their favorite human from room to room. In many cultures, cats have also become symbols of comfort and mystery. From ancient Egyptian temples to modern internet memes, cats have maintained a unique place in human imagination.
